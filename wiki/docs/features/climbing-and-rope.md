Getting up and down a cliff or a tree is possible without a ladder — but climbing is slow, strenuous work that burns stamina and can drop you if you overreach.

## Climbing through leaves

A tree canopy works like **scaffolding**:

- **Stand on top** of a canopy and you can **climb up through it**.
- **Crouch to lower yourself down** through the leaves.

It's **slow** — about **0.4× the speed of a ladder** — and **tiring**, roughly **12 stamina per block**. A **~6 metre tree** eats most of your wind, so don't start a climb on a low bar.

## Free-climbing walls

You can also **scrabble up or down any flat, full-block wall** — no height limit — at a slow rock-climbing pace.

- It is **brutally strenuous**: about **18 stamina per block**, up or down.
- **Run out of stamina mid-climb and you fall.** Never start with an empty bar. See [Survival Meters & HUD](./survival-meters.md) for reading your stamina.
- You **can't climb at all while carrying more than ~25 kg** — drop or store weight first. See [Weight, Carrying & Blocks](./building-and-blocks.md).

Free-climbing is the desperate, last-resort way up. Rope is the civilized one.

## Rope

Rope turns a cliff into a safe, free climb.

- **Craft a coil** from string: **3 string → 3 rope**. (String itself comes from twisting plant fiber — see [Tools & Crafting](./tools-and-crafting.md).)
- **Aim at a block face near the top of a cliff** and use the coil. The rope **unrolls straight down through the open air** — one length per block, up to **32 blocks** deep.
- The hanging rope **climbs exactly like a ladder**: **free, full-speed, and safe** both up and down. No stamina cost, no fall risk.
- **Break the rope** to recover the coil and use it again elsewhere.

If you're going to visit a cliff or a ravine more than once, set a rope. It's the single biggest quality-of-life upgrade over free-climbing.

## Quick comparison

| Method | Speed | Stamina cost | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rope | Full (ladder) | None | Safe |
| Climbing through leaves | ~0.4× ladder | ~12/block | Fall if stamina runs out |
| Free-climbing a wall | Slow | ~18/block | Fall if stamina runs out |

In all cases, **overloading (>~25 kg) blocks climbing entirely** — rope excepted, since rope is treated like a ladder.
